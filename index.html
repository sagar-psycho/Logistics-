FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the transit time for air freight from Bengaluru to Delhi? ⌄
Air freight from Bengaluru (BLR) to Delhi (DEL/IGI) typically takes 2–4 hours for flight transit. Including pick-up, handling, customs, and delivery, door-to-door time is usually 12–24 hours. Same-day delivery is available for bookings confirmed before 10 AM.
What types of cargo can you ship via air freight? ⌄
We handle general cargo, e-commerce shipments, perishables, pharma, high-value goods, automotive parts, electronics, textiles, and hazardous/DG cargo (IATA-certified). Cold chain solutions are available for temperature-sensitive shipments.
How do I track my air freight shipment? ⌄
All shipments booked through Abra E-Logistic come with real-time tracking via our online portal. You'll also receive automated SMS and WhatsApp updates at each milestone — booking confirmed, picked up, departed, arrived, cleared, and delivered.
Do you offer international air freight from Bengaluru? ⌄
Yes. We offer international air freight from Bengaluru to 100+ countries including the UAE, USA, UK, Europe, Singapore, and Africa. Services include export packing, customs clearance, AWB generation, and last-mile delivery at destination.
What documents are needed for international air freight? ⌄
Standard documents required are: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Airway Bill (AWB), Certificate of Origin, and GST/IEC details. For restricted goods, additional permits may apply. Our team handles all documentation for you.
